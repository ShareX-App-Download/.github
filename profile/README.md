## 🚀 Overview
ShareX for Windows is a free, do-it-all capture app that makes screenshots and screen recordings quick and painless. Think of it as a supercharged snipping tool: grab a region, window, or full screen, capture an entire scrolling page in one shot, or record what's happening on your display as video or a GIF. A built-in editor lets you annotate and clean up each capture, and automated workflows handle the repetitive steps for you. With one ShareX download, you get a versatile capture-and-share toolkit that fits everything from quick notes to detailed tutorials.
